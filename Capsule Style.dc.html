Consent — distinct without a hue

A permission ask must stand apart, but Capsule stays near-monochrome: consent is signalled by the uppercase "Permission needed" heading, a ringed "!" glyph, and grey-900 emphasis — never by color alone (so it holds for every kind of colorblindness by construction). Text and controls stay at grey-900 (17.9:1, exceeding AAA 7:1).
